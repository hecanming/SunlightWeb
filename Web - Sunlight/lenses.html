Spherical Lenses

Multiple optical configurations (plano/concave/convex) are manufactured for spherical lenses. Material selection is standardized across K9, UV fused silica, N-SF11, ZnSe, CaF₂, and Si substrates. Wavelength-specific anti-reflection coatings are applied through magnetron sputtering, with operational ranges covering UV (200-400nm), visible (400-700nm), NIR (700-2000nm), and MIR (2-15μm) spectra.

Cylindrical Lenses

Precision cylindrical lenses are engineered for one-dimensional light shaping applications. Available in plano-concave and plano-convex configurations, these optics are fabricated from optical-grade N-BK7 glass, UV fused silica, or CaF2 substrates, with optional anti-reflection coatings to optimize performance.
